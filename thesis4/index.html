About Us

Hello everyone, we are Rex Cedric C. Camia, John Patrick D. Manaig and John Carlo G. Tingson 4th year BS Computer Engineering students from De La Salle University - Dasmarinas. The sole purpose of this web application is for the wearable glove device translator for the hearing and speaking impaired individuals. In this website, the users/patients will be able to see the translation/interpretation depending on the sign language that the glove they wear. Each letter in the American Sign Language except J and Z corresponds to different phrases that are uttered by the hearing and speaking impaired patients. Our group aims to bridge the communication gap between the hearing and speaking impaired and non-impaired individuals, since not all are knowledgeable with sign languages that are used by these patients, therefore, we have created and developed a device in which it can translate a letter from the American Sign Language into a phrase.
